A collection of famous art pieces with comparative perspectives of color blindness.

Let's Begin.


The purpose is to share the different potential-perspectives of famous artworks under different lenses, those representing the different forms of color blindness. I did this to show that the art pieces can be perceived in differently, perhaps in ways that most artists do not even consider themselves.
