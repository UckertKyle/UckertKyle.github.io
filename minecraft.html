Minecraft Sculpture


Within Minecraft I had recreated a piece by Piet Mondrain, and had titled it "This is not a Piet Mondrain." The title was to reflect the fact that the initial piece had been painted on a canvas and this recreation was made out of blocks, but the recreation mirrors the initial geometric appeal of Piet Mondrain's painting. Doing this shows that perhaps the concepts of creations are one in the same.
