Glitch


I wanted to focus on the theme of masks due to it's prevalent appearance in our society these days. The first image was edited in Notepad++ removing, cutting and pasting data while the following two were done in audacity playing with the data with the use of the effect tools. The imagery is intended to be a commentary on recent Covid-19 outbreak and California wildfires.
